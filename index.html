Community Concerns We Handle

Simple reporting for everyday problems affecting people and neighborhoods.

Health

Hospitals, diseases, sanitation issues, and health-related emergencies.

Security

Crime, theft, violence, suspicious activity, and unsafe areas.

Trade

Market problems, unfair prices, seller disputes, and business concerns.

Education

School facilities, teachers, learning environment, and student welfare.

Water

Water shortages, broken taps, and sanitation concerns.

Roads & Electricity

Damaged roads, power outages, and other community infrastructure issues.

Fast, Clean, and Easy Reporting

Citizens can submit issues through a simple form, and authorities can review complaints in one place. The platform is designed to be mobile-friendly, accessible, and easy to use.

  • Responsive on mobile, tablet, and desktop
  • Secure and organized issue submission
  • Clear categories for faster response

How It Works

Three simple steps to report a community problem.

1

Choose a Category

Select health, security, trade, education, or other community issue.

2

Describe the Problem

Explain what happened, where it happened, and what support is needed.

3

Submit the Report

Your issue is sent for review so the right action can be taken.
